Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Founded in 2021 by Tim Stetter, we are dedicated to  serving up rich coffee sourced from artisan farmers in various regions of South and Central America to Ethiopia. We are dedicated to travelling the world, finding the best coffee, and bringing back to you here in our cafe.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
